About the Game

WebXR FPV Shooting Game is an immersive first-person shooter built with A-Frame. It offers eight unique VR environments to explore and test your targeting skills.

The Game Is Packed With 8 Adventurous WebVR Themes of A-Frame For You To Explore The 3D World

How to Play

  • Space - Shoot at targets
  • Arrow Keys - Move forward, backward, left, and right
  • Mouse - Navigate the 3D space
